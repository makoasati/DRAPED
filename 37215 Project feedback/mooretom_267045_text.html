Week 5: Individual Peer Feedback: Tom Moore

The Draped Team has an extremely exciting and well thought out plan for how they are approaching their product design and I believe will create a great product with some further shaping. A few items I wanted to highlight were as follows:

What is the size and nature of the market demand?

I believe you highlighted the potential market size but I believe fleshing out what the TAM may be for this market on South Asian formal wear is and ideally narrowing the market to each of your target cities would be ideal. Knowing this market size is essential as it will help you understand your penetration of the market, as well as how big your marketplace needs to be/should be at launch.

How will you get big fast on both sides?

How are you planning on creating a full marketplace rapidly after your MVP is built? The supply side needs to be rapidly built in order to make this work to accommodate a variety of events as well as body sizes. How will you incentivize suppliers to put their garments up on the market to let strangers use them? Additionally, what protections are you putting in place to support the suppliers (if the garment is damaged) and the users (if the garment doesn’t fit or is not as shown)?

Fit of the Products (Both AR, as well as the products themselves)

The AR functionality was very exciting, but definitely sounds like a complex implementation. Though you mentioned that the sizes of the garments will be intricately input by the suppliers, how will you guarantee that the garments will fit the way they “should,” particularly if they have additional garnishments and features? The AR system should be helpful, but still some of the AR systems at some of the largest companies in America (i.e. Amazon’s “Try it in Your Home”, clothing retailers, etc.) do not work great. How will you create a better system that also implements the intricate details of the supplier’s pieces? Ensure that your tech stack as well as your build time accommodates these considerations.

Additionally, as a side note please implement an understanding of what is culturally appropriate in wearing these pieces for specific occasions. Are certain colors considered faux paus for certain events? Should certain garments be worn in a very specific manner (like how you should never button all 3 buttons on a 3 button suit)? I think a minor education piece would be fantastic.
